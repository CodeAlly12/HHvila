Our Ocean Adventures

Experience the Magic of the Sea

Unforgettable Ocean Journeys

We are offering a range of "full packet" boat and adventure trips. From fishing and grilling at a sandbank, to beach tours with sliding in the sand dunes.

Our boat is newly totally renovated, with a spare motor for safety and trolling, life vests for everyone, secure boat type designed for rough conditions, while we take you enjoying the sea and the shores in Kilifi county (Kilifi, Watamu, Malindi) in a safe and pleasant setting.

All our trips will take you along the shores of Kilifi County. On this trip we are starting in Watamu and going either north to the Mambrui sandunes outside Malindi. On the way we will see some amazing beaches, or try our fishing luck, swim, play in the dunes, or simply enjoy the trip on the Indian Ocean, while we are watching the stunning scenery we are passing.

We do also provide the option of choma (bbq; extra!) or strolling in Malindi town.

Good To Know

Trip Requirements & Recommendations

To ensure you have the best possible experience on our ocean adventures, we recommend the following preparations and requirements for all participants:

Clothing & Protection

Wear comfortable swimwear, bring a change of clothes, sun hat, sunglasses, and apply waterproof sunscreen. We recommend bringing a light jacket for occasional wind.

Swimming Ability

While life jackets are provided, basic swimming ability is recommended for certain activities. Please inform our staff if you are not comfortable in water.

Health Considerations

Inform us of any medical conditions, allergies, or physical limitations before the trip. We recommend bringing any necessary medications.

Food & Beverages

Water is provided, but you may bring additional snacks and drinks. For trips with BBQ options, inform us of any dietary restrictions in advance.

Recommended Items

Consider bringing a waterproof camera, phone case, small towel, and a dry bag for your valuables.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How many people can join the boat trip?

Our boat can comfortably accommodate up to 8 passengers plus our experienced crew. For larger groups, we may be able to arrange multiple boats subject to availability.

Are children allowed on the trips?

Yes, children are welcome on our boat trips. We provide child-sized life jackets for safety. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Some activities may have age restrictions.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Your safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unfavorable, we may reschedule your trip for another day or offer a full refund. We monitor weather conditions closely and will inform you in advance of any changes.

Do I need to bring my own fishing equipment?

No, we provide all necessary fishing equipment including rods, reels, bait, and tackle. You're welcome to bring your own equipment if preferred, but it's not required.

What does the BBQ (choma) option include?

The BBQ option includes a delicious selection of grilled meats (chicken, beef, or fish), accompanied by traditional sides and fresh salads. We can accommodate vegetarian options with advance notice. The BBQ is prepared on a secluded beach or sandbank during your trip.
